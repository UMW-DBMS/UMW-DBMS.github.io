Welcome to Upper Mahaweli Watershed Management Database

Step 1
Select your District & Divisional Secretariat Division name.
If you know the Mini Watershed (MWS) ID, select it. If not, click a location on the map to see its MWS ID in the popup.
Click "GO".
Step 2
Use the footer menu to switch background map (e.g., Satellite, OSM).
Step 3
Select "Base Layers" to explore GIS data layers. Select "Attribute" to view detailed information by clicking features on the map.
Step 4
Select "Proposal" to explore proposal locations.
Step 5
Select "Climate Layer" for weather data and use "Attribute" for time-series exploration.
Step 6
To see the water quality station information click on "Water Quality Data". Click on the each Station point to see more information
Step 7
Select "MWS Info" for watershed committee details.
Step 8
Select "Data Catalog" to access metadata and downloads. You can go to the "Open MWS Data Catalog" to access and downloads.
Step 9
Select "Statistics" for MWS-level statistics (e.g., land cover, population).
Step 10
To explore another MWS, close panels, change the MWS ID, and click "GO".
